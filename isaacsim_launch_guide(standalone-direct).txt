===================================================================================
This guide is to launch Isaac Sim 4.0.0 standalone directly from the isaac-sandbox.
===================================================================================

cd ~

cd isaac-docker/isaac-sandbox/docker

./run_docker.sh 

./open_isaac.sh


docker pull gitlab.cerlab.lan.cmu.edu:5050/cerlab_public/isaac-sandbox:Ubuntu-20.04_Isaac-4.5.0_Cuda-12.2.0
